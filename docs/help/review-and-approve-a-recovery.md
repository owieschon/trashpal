# Review and approve a recovery

Use this page to decide whether to approve the exact recovery Pal prepared for one case.

## Review the whole recovery

Approval is for a specific recovery, not a general permission for Pal to act. Before approving, check:

- the site, service need, and recovery deadline;
- the confirmed access window;
- the proposed vehicle and service time;
- the evidence behind those details; and
- any uncertainty or exception Pal has called out.

If any part is wrong, incomplete, expired, or outside your authority, do not approve it. Resolve the missing information or use your normal escalation path instead.

## What approval means

When you approve, you approve the exact recovery you reviewed. TrashPal checks that the case is still current before it creates the dispatch operation. If the evidence, access, or timing has changed, the recovery may no longer be valid and needs a fresh review.

Pal prepares the recommendation. The dispatcher makes the approval decision. Approval does not mean the collection is complete.

## After approval

Follow the operation status. A dispatch request can become uncertain when an outside system accepts work but its response does not reach TrashPal. In that case, do not submit the recovery again. Go to [check an uncertain dispatch](check-an-uncertain-dispatch.md).
