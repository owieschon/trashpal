# Start here: resolve a missed collection safely

TrashPal helps dispatch teams work through a service exception without treating uncertain facts as settled.

Pal reviews the active case and prepares a recommendation from the records it is allowed to use. You stay in control: inspect the recommendation, approve the exact recovery when it is right, and follow the operation until its status is clear.

## Follow this path

1. [Resolve a missed collection](resolve-a-missed-collection.md) to understand the case and what Pal needs before it can prepare a recovery.
2. [Review and approve a recovery](review-and-approve-a-recovery.md) to make a decision about the specific work Pal prepared.
3. [Check an uncertain dispatch](check-an-uncertain-dispatch.md) when the dispatch system's response is incomplete or missing.
4. [Read a recovery receipt](read-a-recovery-receipt.md) to understand the record that follows a recovery.

## What Pal does

Pal gathers the relevant service, access, and route information for one case. It can point out missing or conflicting information and prepare a recovery recommendation when the requirements are met.

Pal prepares recommendations. A dispatcher approves the exact recovery. Pal does not decide that a collection happened, change a service agreement, or make a customer promise on its own.
