# Resolve a missed collection

Use this page to turn a reported missed collection into a clear, reviewable recovery decision.

## Start with the case

Open the reported collection and confirm that it belongs to the right customer site. Read the latest field attempt and the customer or site update together. A report that access is clear is useful, but it does not erase a more recent conflicting field report.

## Let Pal prepare the case

Pal checks the information needed for a safe recovery:

- What happened on the original collection attempt.
- Whether access is confirmed for a specific time.
- What the service agreement allows and when the recovery is due.
- Whether a suitable recovery option is available within those limits.

If a required fact is missing, stale, or conflicts with another record, Pal should stop and explain what needs confirmation. That is a useful result. Do not treat an incomplete recommendation as permission to send work.

## Read the recommendation

When Pal can prepare a recovery, read its evidence and the proposed service window before making any decision. The recommendation should make clear:

- the reason for the recovery;
- the records that support it;
- anything that remains unknown or conflicted; and
- the work it is asking a dispatcher to approve.

Continue to [review and approve a recovery](review-and-approve-a-recovery.md) only when the case has a specific recovery ready for review. If the case is still missing information, keep the case open and obtain the needed confirmation through your normal operating process.
